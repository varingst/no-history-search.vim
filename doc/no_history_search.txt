*no_history_search.txt*  Interactive search without clogging history

MAPPINGS                                     *no_history_search-mappings*

<Plug>(no-history-/)           Like |/|
<Plug>(no-history-?)           Like |?|

OPTIONS                                      *no_history_search-options*

g:no_history_search_prepend = {string}    or
b:no_history_search_prepend = {string}

Prepends the {string} to the search. I like "\<" |/\<|
